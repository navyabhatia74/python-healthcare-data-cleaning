# Project Insights

## Appointment Attendance Trends

The majority of healthcare appointments in the dataset were successfully attended, while a smaller percentage resulted in missed appointments. Attendance analysis helped identify behavioral and operational patterns that can affect healthcare scheduling efficiency.

---

## Gender-Based Analysis

Female patients accounted for a larger share of appointments compared to male patients. Attendance behavior between genders remained relatively consistent, although slight variations in missed appointment percentages were observed.

---

## Age Group Insights

Adult patients formed the largest patient segment within the dataset. Younger and middle-aged groups showed higher appointment activity, while senior citizens displayed comparatively different attendance behavior patterns. Age-group segmentation helped improve demographic-level analysis.

---

## Waiting Time Impact

One of the strongest observations from the analysis was the relationship between waiting time and missed appointments. Patients with longer waiting periods between scheduling and appointment dates showed a higher probability of not attending appointments. Shorter waiting periods generally resulted in improved attendance rates.

---

## Weekday Appointment Patterns

Appointment volume varied across weekdays, with certain days showing significantly higher scheduling activity. Attendance patterns also changed slightly depending on the appointment day, highlighting the importance of operational planning in healthcare scheduling systems.

---

## Data Cleaning & Preprocessing Learnings

The project highlighted the importance of preprocessing before analysis. Tasks such as missing value analysis, duplicate handling, datetime conversion, feature engineering, and data validation were essential in preparing the dataset for reliable analytics and visualization workflows.

---

## Overall Project Outcome

This project demonstrated how Python can be used to transform raw healthcare data into meaningful business and operational insights through structured preprocessing, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis, and visualization workflows using pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn.
